Module 5: Images & Videos

Learning Objectives

In this module, you will learn to:

  • Add images and videos to your HTML pages
  • Manage uploads in your FTP or GitHub folder

Watch

Tutorial: Images and Videos

Attached Files:

  • IDN 530 SP18 - M6.pptx
  • m6.docx

Do

  • Add an image (or more) to your existing web pages
  • Consider adding your own photo to intro.html, or food photos to recipe.html
  • Add an image that links to another page
  • Add a self-hosted or embedded video to one of your pages
  • Use width="%” to control the size of images and videos
  • Upload all images and modified HTML files to FTP or GitHub
  • Submit the public link to your updated site
