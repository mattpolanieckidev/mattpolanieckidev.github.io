Privacy Policy

Shabbos Times Chrome Extension

Data Collection & Storage

We respect your privacy and believe in transparency. Here's what you need to know:

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

Local Storage Only

The extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Zip Code: Your last entered zip code for quick access

This data never leaves your computer and is only stored in your browser's local storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following external services:

  • Hebcal API: For Shabbos times and Torah portion data

Your zip code is sent to Hebcal to fetch relevant Shabbos times, but we don't store this information.

Your Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Use different zip codes at any time

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store.
